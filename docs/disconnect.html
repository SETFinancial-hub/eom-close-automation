Disconnect QuickBooks Access

To revoke the SET Financial — QuickBooks MCP Server application's access to your QuickBooks Online account, follow the steps below.

Steps to Disconnect:
  1. Log in to your QuickBooks Online account.
  2. Click the Settings gear icon (top right).
  3. Under Lists, select Apps.
  4. Find SET Financial — QuickBooks MCP Server in the Connected Apps list.
  5. Click Disconnect and confirm.

Once disconnected, this application will no longer have access to your QuickBooks Online data. Any locally cached data will be cleared on the next application restart.
